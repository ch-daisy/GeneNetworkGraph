You can use Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox or Microsoft Internet Explorer (v9 or above) to access the Gene Network
Gene Network
This is a subset of the gene network, Genes relate to each other
  • Hover over a node to see the gene title, and to highlight the genes related to it.
  • Click on a node to show gene details. It will open up a side panel.
  • On the gene details panel, you can click on the search icon to center the graph on that movie. And clicking on the link to a related gene will move to that gene (in the graph and in the panel)
  • You can use you usual browser controls to zoom and pan over the graph. If you zoom in when centred on a gene, the graph will expand around it.
  • Above a certain zoom level, gene titles are automatically displayed for all the nodes.
